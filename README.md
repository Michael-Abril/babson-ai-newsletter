## Why This Exists

Most people use AI as a fancy search engine. Open ChatGPT, ask a question, close it. But AI can do so much more: validate a business idea in a weekend, automate 80% of your market research, build an MVP without a dev team, and create investor-ready pitch decks in hours instead of weeks.

The AI Pulse exists to close that gap. Each issue is written specifically for Babson students and filtered through the lens of entrepreneurship. This is how you actually learn to use AI, not just chat with it.
